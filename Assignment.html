Puppies and the 'mesh' network

You may enjoy synchronzied swimming, puppies enjoy 'synchronized pooping'

I have observed in my 5 weeks (thus far) of being a guardian to 7 lovely puppies that they seem to have some 'unspoken' level of communication amongst them that is perhaps below the level of their own awareness. This phenomenon I term a 'mesh network'. Puppies demonstrate this phenomenon when defecating: one puppy 'initializes' this mesh activity which then spurs all the other puppies into the same activity at roughly the same time : and voi la, you end up having all puppies defecating in unison. The same sort of 'mesh synchronicity' is observed when they urinate as well.

What is the 'ether' of the 'Mesh Network'?

Clearly this is not a mere coincidence, as I've surmised in my earlier paragraph, but rather there must be some entity or entities that are transmitted amongst the puppies that would trigger this chain reaction of elimination activities (pooping and peeing). I would hazard to say that it is likely a type of pheremone some such scent-based particle that is present in the eliminated matter that is then detected by adjacent puppies and so triggers the chain reaction.
